Project Description

The project will produce a website which houses an interactive map-based timeline. The map-based timeline will highlight and memorialize the history of past human rights violations and events that took place in Canada, Eastern Europe, and Africa. Living Rights Legacy adds a digital element to the exhibit and bridges the gap between how different generations consume content/knowledge by presenting an alternative engagement opportunity that brings contemporary perspectives in conversations around the subject. By including the voices and lived-experiences of survivors, Living Rights Legacy seeks to promote a safe place where visitors can explore incidents where people’s human rights were violated.
