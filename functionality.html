Functionality and Technology

When the user visits the Living Rights Legacy website, there will be a pop up box that introduces the summary and navigation elements. Ex. “Welcome to Living Rights Legacy, an interactive map based timeline displaying a contemporary perspective on human rights. Watch the timeline play through the events surrounding human rights history around the world. Listen, read and replay at your own pace.” The pop up welcome box is able to be minimized in the upper corner for the user to refer back to if necessary.

Other Features Include

  • World Map
  • Timeline
  • Framework
